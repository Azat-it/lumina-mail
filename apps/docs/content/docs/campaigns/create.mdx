## Step 1: Start a New Campaign

Click "Create Campaign" in the dashboard and you will be redirected to the campaign creation page.

<ImageZoom src="/create-campaign.png" width="1200" height="1200" alt="Campaign creation page" />

## Step 2: Campaign Settings

Fill in the basic campaign details:

- Subject line
- Email title (optional)
- Call to action button text and link (optional)
- Email content

<ImageZoom src="/campaign-editor.png" width="1200" height="1200" alt="Campaign settings" />

## Step 3: Test Your Campaign (optional)

<ImageZoom src="/save-draft-campaign.png" width="1200" height="1200" alt="Campaign settings" />

Before sending:

1. Save the campaign as a draft
2. Preview your campaign if everything as you expected


## Step 4: Send

Options for sending:

- Send immediately from editor
- Send immediately from campaings page

<Callout type="warn">
  Once sent, campaigns cannot be edited or deleted from your campaign listeners. Double-check everything before
  sending!
</Callout>
